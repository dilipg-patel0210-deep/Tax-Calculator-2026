# Tax Calculator 2026

This guide will help you understand how to install and use the Progressive Web App (PWA) version of the Tax Calculator 2026 on mobile devices.

## PWA Installation Guide
1. **Open the browser:** Use a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
2. **Visit the app URL:** Navigate to [Tax Calculator 2026](https://your-app-url.com).
3. **Install the PWA:**  
   - On Android: Tap the menu button in your browser and look for the "Add to Home Screen" option.  
   - On iOS: Tap the Share button and select "Add to Home Screen".
4. **Access the app:** The app icon will now appear on your home screen, and you can open it like a native app.

## Mobile App Usage Guide
- **Navigating the app:** Use the home screen for quick access to features.  
- **Calculating taxes:** Enter your details in the respective fields and tap the "Calculate" button.  
- **Viewing results:** The tax results will be displayed instantly on the screen.  
- **Saving results:** You can save your calculations for future reference.
